title: "Forgejo is now copyleft, just like Git"
url: "https://forgejo.org/2024-08-gpl/"
date: "2024-08-22"
feed_url: "https://forgejo.org/rss.xml"
---
In June 2023, Forgejo agreed to allow copyleft code to be merged in the codebase. A year later, in August 2024, the first pull requests to take advantage of this opportunity were merged. Forgejo versions starting from v9.0 are now released under a copyleft license and earlier Forgejo versions, including v8.0 and v7.0 patch releases remain under the MIT license.
