Contributors got together to celebrate the release of Forgejo v7.0 and Codeberg was upgraded the next day. A lot of effort went into the automation of the development process, for dependency management and releases, so that contributors can focus on what matters most. As Forgejo matures, more and more of the work is about day to day management of bug reports, localization, security, etc.
